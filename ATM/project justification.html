Project Justification

Workload

The pre-arrangement of our group is that each person will work one hour a day, and each person will work a total of five hours a week. In the process of doing homework, we made appropriate adjustments. Everyone worked five hours a week, and a meeting was held on Sunday to discuss the problems encountered during homework. And we divided the work within the group. The two were a group, and the assignment was divided into three parts: the web part and two documents. Each group chooses a main part and assists other groups. Each part should be completed by August 9th, and the last meeting will be held on August 9th, integrating the three parts, and in the last week, make the final revision and submit.

Beyond Current Capabilities

Each of us is good at writing documents. For coding, everyone has different skills. Some people are good at css, some are good at html, and some are good at java. In our group, we are divided into three groups, two people make a group, each of the two people in the group is good at different skills, some people are good at writing code, some people are good at writing documents, people who are good at writing code , Will mainly do code work, and those who are good at writing documents will assist those who write code and do some code work, and those who are good at writing code will also do some documents work, so that everyone will get out of their comfort zone , And learn different skills from other team members to improve their IT skills.

Risks

Risk: AWS is not good at using
Example: Everyone in our group is using AWS for the first time and is better at using GitHub.
Mitigation: We discussed this issue with the teacher, and the teacher sent us a usage report, which helped us a lot. Everyone in the group teaches the others what he know.

Risk: Different opinions
Example: At the beginning of the assignment, everyone has a different idea of the procedure.
Mitigation: We have elected a team leader who will remove bad ideas and integrate good ideas. Because sometimes ideas that we can't complete are presented, the project will be difficult to proceed without the team leader.

Risk: The project is very difficult
Example: This project involves many aspects and is very difficult.
Mitigation: The realization of this project will involve many different fields. For the first part of the homework, we can also solve it. When the project is in the later stage, it is difficult for our existing knowledge to meet our goals, so in our spare time, they are all trying to supplement their knowledge to ensure the successful completion of the project.
